KASHMIR

Kashmir, often called “Paradise on Earth,” is famous for its breathtaking valleys, snow-covered mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and serene landscapes. Surrounded by the majestic Himalayas, Kashmir offers a perfect escape into nature’s beauty and tranquility. Visitors can enjoy peaceful shikara rides on Dal Lake, explore colorful gardens, and experience snowfall in the beautiful hill stations. Adventure lovers can enjoy trekking, skiing, gondola rides, and mountain adventures. With its stunning scenery, rich culture, warm hospitality, and unforgettable views, Kashmir promises a magical experience for every traveler.
